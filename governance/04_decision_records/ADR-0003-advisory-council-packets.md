# ADR-0003: Advisory Council Packets

Status: Approved
Date: 2026-05-24
Operator: Jeremy

## Decision

Kimi, Qwen, and DeepSeek will receive copy-paste advisory packets when Jeremy starts a new online chat for outside review. Each advisory member receives shared AXIOM context plus their own role instructions.

All advisory responses must be returned in markdown file format.

## Reason

Jeremy does not have terminal access for Kimi, Qwen, or DeepSeek. Each new chat needs enough context to produce useful advice without depending on previous chat memory.

## Impact

Advisory council files live in `governance/03_advisory_council/`. Advisory responses remain non-binding until Jeremy accepts and records them through the AXIOM decision workflow.

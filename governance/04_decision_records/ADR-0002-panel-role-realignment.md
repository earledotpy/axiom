# ADR-0002: Panel Role Realignment

Status: Approved
Date: 2026-05-24
Operator: Jeremy

## Decision

Codex / GPT-5.5 is assigned the role of Implementation Specialist and Troubleshooter.

Claude Code / Claude is assigned the role of Governance Auditor and Specification Critic.

Antigravity / Gemini 3.5 is assigned the role of Chief Architect and Researcher.

Jeremy remains the Operator and final authority.

## Reason

Codex has been most useful for implementation and troubleshooting. Antigravity is expected to be better suited for architecture and research because of its search, reasoning, and design-analysis strengths. Claude Code remains best positioned to audit governance coherence and specification quality.

## Impact

Panel members now have clearer ownership boundaries. Codex should implement and verify. Claude Code should critique and audit. Antigravity should research and architect. Jeremy decides what becomes binding.

## 11. Retroactive 30-Day Audit Applicability — Inheritance Record

**For inheritance by future Synthesis documents:**

The retroactive 30-day audit clause in Charter v1.1 §Charter Amendment Process (operationalized in §2.2) becomes operative upon ratification of this v1.0 → v1.1 amendment package.

**Scope rules:**

1. The audit clause has **no retroactive scope to v1.0 → v1.1 itself.** The very amendment cycle that ratifies the audit clause is exempt from being its first audit subject. (Per `AXIOM_Synthesis_Governance_v2.md` §10 inheritance record.)
2. The first audit-eligible Charter amendment is the one that **follows** v1.1 ratification (i.e., a hypothetical v1.1 → v1.2 governance amendment, or whatever the next Charter-touching amendment is named).
3. Per Charter v1.1 §2.1 (the D5 closure), the audit is **prospective-only** even when active: it may flag prior decisions but cannot retroactively reopen them without a new panel motion and full panel consensus.
4. The audit artifact format is `AXIOM_Charter_Amendment_Audit_<YYYYMMDD>.md` per Charter v1.1 §2.2.

Any future proposal that attempts to apply the 30-day audit retroactively to v1.0 → v1.1 should be flagged as a Charter §Integration Discipline violation by the Evaluator at that future cycle.
